## ‚úçÔ∏è Writing Great Essays

### Title Best Practices

**Do:**
- Keep titles under 60 characters
- Make them descriptive and compelling
- Use action words when appropriate
- Be specific about the topic

**Examples:**
- ‚úÖ "The Rise of African Innovation in FinTech"
- ‚úÖ "How Mobile Money is Transforming Rural Economies"
- ‚ùå "Essay About Africa" (too vague)
- ‚ùå "An Extremely Long Title That Goes On Forever And Never Seems To End" (too long)

### Description Writing

The description appears on essay cards. Make it count!

**Guidelines:**
- 150-200 characters ideal
- Summarize the main point
- Create curiosity
- Avoid clickbait

**Example:**
```
"Exploring how Kenya's mobile banking revolution is inspiring 
fintech innovation across the African continent and beyond."
```

### Content Structure

Use HTML tags for formatting:

```html
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Your opening paragraph that hooks the reader...</p>

<h2>Main Point 1</h2>
<p>Supporting details and examples...</p>

<h2>Main Point 2</h2>
<p>More insights and analysis...</p>

<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>Wrap up your thoughts and provide takeaways...</p>
```

### Formatting Tips

**Headings:**
```html
<h2>Section Title</h2>  <!-- Use for main sections -->
<h3>Subsection</h3>      <!-- Use for subsections -->
```

**Text Formatting:**
```html
<strong>Bold text</strong>
<em>Italic text</em>
<a href="https://example.com">Link text</a>
```

**Lists:**
```html
<ul>
  <li>Bullet point 1</li>
  <li>Bullet point 2</li>
</ul>

<ol>
  <li>Numbered item 1</li>
  <li>Numbered item 2</li>
</ol>
```

**Quotes:**
```html
<blockquote>
  <p>"A powerful quote that emphasizes your point."</p>
  <cite>- Author Name</cite>
</blockquote>
```

## üöÄ Launch Your First Essay

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Choose a strong topic** you're passionate about
2. **Outline main points** before writing
3. **Write your first draft** without editing
4. **Let it sit** for a few hours or overnight
5. **Edit and refine** with fresh eyes
6. **Add formatting** for readability
7. **Upload thumbnail** that captures attention
8. **Add tags** for discoverability
9. **Preview** before publishing
10. **Publish** and share on social media!

## üí™ Overcoming Writer's Block

**When stuck:**
- Read other essays for inspiration
- Brainstorm with bullet points first
- Start with what you know best
- Write the middle first, then intro/conclusion
- Take a break and come back fresh
- Talk through your ideas aloud

**Quick Prompts:**
- "The most surprising thing about [topic] is..."
- "Three misconceptions about [topic]..."
- "What I learned from [experience]..."
- "Why [topic] matters now more than ever..."
